There is always something to do at the Lucchi Family Wine Cellars.

We hold events for all occasions and holidays--from wine & design to live music and more.

Don't miss out!

Follow us on facebook and Instagram to stay up to date on all upcoming events! We invite local bands and support local artists at our events and in our merchandise sold at the winery. Come in and join us!

We make appearances at market and street festivals all across the state of Pennsylvania--if you can't make it into our Scranton location, just look for us at your annual beer, wine, or arts festival!

Not only do we believe in supporting local artists and local communities, but we also believe in advocating for social causes that are important to us--this junior baseball team is wearing Lucchi Family Wine Cellars' uniforms.
